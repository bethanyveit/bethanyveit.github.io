Canvas

Canvas is your tool for viewing all of your classes that you're currently enrolled in. Depending on your professors usage, you will be able to see and turn in assign,ents, view grades, message your professors and classmates directly, and view an online calendar of upcoming assignments. Please remember to signout using the "LOGOUT" link in Canvas. SIgning out of MyMadison will NOT sign you out of Canvas.
