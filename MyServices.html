Card Services

JMU Card Services
Through the JMU Card Services website, students are able to choose or change a meal plan, add money onto their dining dollars GOLD account, add moeny to their FLEX account, and check to see how many remaining punches they have left for the week as well as see their dining dollars and FLEX balances.

Parking Services

JMU Parking Services
Through the JMU Parking Services website, students are able to see parking pass options as well as purchase online, pay a citation, see a map, and locate which lots/garages they are able to park in with their specific pass.

Dining Services

JMU Dining Services
Students are able to purchase their meal plans, check menus for the day with nutrition facts, see hours of operation, and much more.

Medical Services

JMU Medical Services
At JMU, students have access to an on-campus medical facility. With the link above, they can book/see appointments, check services and costs, talk to a nurse, learn about self care, and more.
