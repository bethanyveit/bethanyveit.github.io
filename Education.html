Loudoun County Highschool

I attended Loudoun County Highschool in Leesburg, Virginia from 2013-2017. I was on the varsity cheerleading team for freshman, sophomore, and senior year. Everyone says junior year of highschool is the harded and I wanted to focus on classes and cheerleading is a very demanding sport. However, during the spring sports season of junior year, I tried gymnastics as I had gotten comfortable with my school work and didn't want to not participate in a sport. My senior year at LCHS, I joined the newspaper staff. I ended up becoming the A&E editor. I really enjoyed the job and it piqued my interest in journalism. However, I found more joy in designing the layout of the pages which is how I knew the School of Media Art and Design (SMAD) was the right one to apply for at James Madison Univeristy (JMU).

James Madison University

Transitioning from highschool to JMU was defintiely very daunting and intimidating. I had never really been apart from my parents, and being two hours away from my house at the time seemed like a scary, but also exciting new adventure. I decided not to try out for the varsity cheer team going into college as I knew it was a large commitment I wasn't sure if I was ready for. Instead, I joined the club cheerleading team where I made a lot of my clsoe friends. I did club for two years and although I lvoed it, going into my junior year I knew that I wanted to tryout for the varsity team, so I did. Joining varsity cheer at JMU was one of the best decisions I ever made. I made so many new friends and love every second of cheering on the school. the major of SMAD also was a perfect fit. Doing what I enjoy for a major make my projects not feel like work, but enjoyment.
