About

Thank you for visiting my site! My name is Bethany Veit and I am currently a senior at James Madison University and pursuing a Bachelors of Art from the School of Media Art and Design with a concentration in Interactive Design. I am on the Varsity Coed Cheerleading team so during the fall you can find me on the sidelines of the football field and in the spring I am cheering on our basketball teams. Although that was halted for my first semester of senior year due to Covid-19, during the spring semester I was able to experience the best of both worlds cheering with my fellow seniors at both football and basketball games.

During my early childhood, I lived in Massachusetts, but moved down to Northern Virginia, where I currently live, when I was around 12 years old. I started working when I was 16 years old and I am forever grateful to have learned customer service skills at such a young age. Over the summer of 2019 I got a glimpse into the working world as I was hired as a summer technology intern by Volkswagen of America in Reston, VA. I helped design an online interactive handbook, create logos, shoot videos, and design a new online learning module.

I am a very hard working individual and take pride in the work that I complete. I hope that you can see that being reflected as you browse around my site.
