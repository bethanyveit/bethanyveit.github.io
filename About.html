About Me

Designer

Developer

Athlete

In May 2021, I graduated from James Madison University with a Bachelor of Arts in Media Art and Design - Interactive Design. I was a part of the varsity coed cheerleading team, and nothing will beat my memories of flying above the stands while getting to cheer on my fellow Dukes. I love being outdoors whether it's hiking, swimming, or just enjoying the fresh air over some drinks with friends and/or family. I also love to travel, see new places, and try new things!


I moved to Northern Virginia from Massachusetts when I was around 11 years old, and have been in the area ever since, however, I can't wait to get out and explore more of the U.S while also developing my resume and pursuing a great career. I'm hoping to one day work in the graphic design or front-end development field since that's where my interests mainly lie, but UX/UI design is also very intriguing to me.


As you will see on my resume, I started working when I was 16 years old and have always had a summer job since then. Throughout highschool I worked a customer service job and am more than grateful that I got that experience at such a young age. My first real-world work experience came over the summer of 2019 when I got to intern for Volkswagen of America. It was such a great hand-on learning experience to take what I had learned in the classroom and apply it to real life.


In the near future I hope to find a place of my own, for the moment I'm searching for my first solo apartment. I also hope to one day find a job that I truly enjoy, not just in the workplace envionment but also in the work itself, I want a job that I am passionate and excited about. My third goal is to be financially stable enough to support myself, my hamster Crouton, and any other furry friends that join my little family.
